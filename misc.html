Pokémon Information

Each Pokémon is assigned one to two elemental types that influence its development, abilities, and moves it can learn. These types play a significant role in defining a Pokémon’s strengths and weaknesses. Similar to real-world animals, Pokémon fall within specific ranges of height and weight, although most species are represented by standard values for these attributes. These statistics are included in Pokédex entries.

Habitats

Pokémon inhabit a range of environments, including caves, rivers, forests, mountains, and fields. These habitats typically correspond with a Pokémon’s elemental type. For example, water type Pokémon are commonly found in oceans or rivers, while grass type Pokémon are more likely to appear in meadows or forested areas. In the Pokémon video games, a Pokémon’s habitat determines where it can be encountered, making location an important factor in gameplay.
