Updates

The Pokémon featured on this page come from the original Pokémon games, released in 1996. Since then, the franchise has expanded significantly, with each new generation introducing additional Pokémon species. Each subsequent release has continued this trend, adding new species and expanding the Pokédex. Future additions will be added systematically based on their original release order, ensuring consistency with how they were introduced across games and other media.

Page Purpose and Functionality

This page was designed to display and organize detailed information about Pokémon in a clear and accessible format. The interface is inspired by the design of the Pokédex seen in the Pokémon games. Structured elements such as tables and lists are used to organize information efficiently. My goal was to create a layout that is both visually intuitive and easy to navigate.

Pokédex Numbering System

Within the Pokémon franchise, each Pokémon is assigned an identification number that reflects its position in the Pokédex. These numbers provide a standardized method for organizing and referencing Pokémon across media. Pokémon that are part of the same evolutionary line are typically positioned sequentially, allowing users to easily identify relationships between species.
