About Me

Hi there, my name is Wex. I am a third year ICT student at the University of Kentucky.

I have been to Lexington, Chicago, and Los Angeles.

This semester I have been assigned a project where I create a multiple page website that interacts with the user. We were given creative freedom regarding what kind of webite we create; what I plan to do is create a functional pokedex with integrated sounds and graphics. For those of you who don't know what a pokedex, it is an encyclopedia from the pokemon franchise which stores information about all of the pokemon including their cries, appearance, color, size, etc. I want to have all of this information in my pokedex. It is also a goal of mine to make the website look as accurate to the pokedex in the pokemon games as possible. I will be including the original 151 pokemon and will be basing the pokedex off of the one in the Heartgold and Soulsilver games, which came out on September 12th, 2009. I want to include a 3d model graphic for each pokemon.
