Pokemon Heights, Weights, and Types

In the pokemon franchise, pokemon are creatures of various shapes and sizes that inhabit the world. Each pokemon has 1 or 2 elemental types, of which there are 18; The types they are largely determine what moves the can learn and what abilities they have. Since pokemon are typically based on animals, just like animals, they have predetermined heights and weights that can vary by small margins. I will list the height and weight range of each pokemon. In the pokemon franchise, the pokedex is an electronic dictionary with descriptions of each pokemon and how they typically act. I will be uploading these descriptions to this page with the intention of recreating the pokedex in the form of a website.

Pokemon Habitats

In the fictional pokemon world, pokemon, just like animals, live in a variety of habitats such as oceans, caves, fields, mountains, etc. These habitats often happen to line up with the pokemon in question's elemental type. For example, a pokemon with the elemental type water may live in oceans or rivers, while a pokemon with the grass or normal type may live in fields or meadows. These habitats influence how you can encounter pokemon in games; you need to find the suitable habitat for the pokemon you are looking for, and go there. In my complete pokedex, I want to include a short description of what kinds of habitats said pokemon live in. I will determine this based on their elemental types.

Pokemon Evolution

Another aspect of the pokemon franchise which I have not covered so far is pokemon evolution; in the pokemon franchise, once a pokemon has gained enough battle experience it "evolves" into a new pokemon. this is not like traditional evolution where species grow new traits over time based on natural selection and adaptability, but rather just a change in forms. Not all pokemon have evolutions, and only have one form. When I complete this pokedex, I want to make it so that you can tell what pokemon evolve and which pokemon evolve into.
